Development of the K-pop IDOL industry

BIGBANG



BIGBANG officially made their debut as a 5-member group in late December 2006, through the album
BIGBANG Vol.1 – Since 2007. The album came after the pre-release of 3 singles– We Belong Together,
A Fool’s Only Tears, and This Love.

After a two-year hiatus in South Korea, the quintet returned with their fourth EP,
Tonight (2011), won them the inaugural Best Worldwide Act Award at the 2011
MTV Europe Music Awards.

BIGBANG’s 5th Korean EP Alive (2012) was popular internationally.
Tracks like Blue, Bad Boy, and Fantastic Baby topped Korean charts and
even entered the Billboard 200 charts. And later tour, attracted 1.5 million people and
set a new record for the most-attended concert tour by a Korean act in history at the time.
Despite various member's scandals, their songs are well-loved still now.
