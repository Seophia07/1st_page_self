2AM



2AM is a South Korean boy band, that consists of Jo Kwon, Lee Changmin, Lim Seulong
and Jeong Jinwoon. The band was formed by JYP Entertainment through the Mnet documentary
Hot Blood, which spawned the eleven-member boy band One Day.

2AM primarily perform ballad songs while their brother group 2PM performed more dance-focused.
With the addition of Changmin from Big Hit Entertainment, the group was co-managed by JYP and
Big Hit from 2008 to 2014. They officially debuted on July 11, 2008 on KBS' Music Bank,
performing their debut single "This Song".

Later, Several of their singles have achieved high peaks at the Gaon Digital Chart,
with "Can't Let You Go Even If I Die", "You Wouldn't Answer My Calls", and
"I Wonder If You Hurt Like Me" topping the chart.
Their Japanese singles have also peaked within the top ten of the Oricon Singles Chart.
