Development of the K-pop IDOL industry

Super Junior




Super Junior is also dubbed by the media as the "King of Hallyu Wave" due to their prominent
contributions in Korean Wave. They debuted on November 6, 2005, "Twins (Knock Out)"

They consisted of thirteen members in 2005. But later, Kyuhyun joined the group in 2006.
And Han kyeng and Kibum had left SM Enter&Super Junior in 2009 and 2015.

They're active currently. After more than 15 years in the K-pop game,
Super Junior is here to reassure fans the boy band has no intention of leaving.
In fact, their new music is coming in multiple chapters to prove the point.
